About the Project

ChaosEncrypt is a cutting-edge encryption system that leverages chaos theory for secure communication. Our implementation uses the logistic map's chaotic properties to generate unpredictable yet deterministic sequences for encryption.

The project began as an exploration of applying mathematical concepts to cryptography, evolving into a robust encryption solution that combines theoretical foundations with practical security measures.
